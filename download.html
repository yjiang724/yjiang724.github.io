Datasets download

  • viral_gene_fasta.tar.gz: 3,351,765 viral gene sequences detected in metagenomics datasets of five diseases and the corresponding healthy controls.
  • viral_gene_annotation.tar.gz: Annotations for each viral gene including taxonomy, KEGG, ACLAME, and VirGenFunD category.
  • family_annotation.txt: The functional annotation of ACLAME viral protein families.
  • function_category.txt: The mapping file of functional annotation to VirGenFunD categories.
  • cate-function.txt: The functional annotation of each VirGenFunD category.
